Life in Weeks Coding Exercise

I was reading this article by Tim Urban - Your Life in Weeks and realised just how little time we actually have.

In this Challenge, youre going to create a page that tells you how many days, weeks, and months you have left if you live until 90 years old.


It will take your current age as input and Console logs a message with our time left in this format:

You have x days, y weeks, and z months left.

where x, y, and z are replaced with the actual calculated numbers.


For this challenge, assume there are 365 days in a year, 52 weeks in a year and 12 months in a year.
