Welcome to Punjab University, Gujranwala Campus

Established to enhance educational access and quality, our campus is dedicated to fostering a vibrant learning environment.

  • State-of-the-art facilities and resources.
  • A diverse range of programs across various disciplines.
  • Experienced faculty committed to student success.

Located near Alipur Chowk, Rawalpindi By-pass, our campus is designed to support both academic and extracurricular pursuits, ensuring a well-rounded educational experience. We invite you to join us in this journey of growth and discovery.
