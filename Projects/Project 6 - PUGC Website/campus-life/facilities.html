Campus Facilities

Multipurpose Hall

A spacious hall for seminars, conferences, and events held on campus.

Cricket/Football Ground

A large ground for sports activities including cricket and football tournaments.

Computer Labs

State-of-the-art labs equipped with the latest technology for computer science and IT students.

Library

A vast collection of academic books, research papers, and journals for students and faculty.

Regional Plan 9 Incubation Center

A space for student entrepreneurs to incubate their startups and receive mentorship.

E-Rozgar Technical Training Center

A government-run center providing technical training to students for freelancing and career development.

Cafeteria

A hygienic and spacious place where students can enjoy meals and snacks during breaks.

Medical Dispensary

On-campus medical dispensary for first aid and basic healthcare services.

Car Parking

Secure parking area for students, staff, and visitors within the campus.
